About Football Blackjack

Tim came up with a great way to lose money, so we thought we would give it a go.

Rules

Get a randomly selected team at the start of each round.
Land on exactly 11 league goals.
If your team goes over, you start again at 0.
Only league games count.
If you win you get £1 per player per week.
Give £5 per month to Simon in Process
